Begining Stages

The following screens will help you understand the processes of the app, please click Next to begin.

Unregisterd User

If you're an unregisterd user you can still see tournaments and all your favourite teams participating in them, however, you will not be able to participate yourself.

Team Manager

If you register as a team manager, your account and your team will need to be approved by the admin before you can apply to any tournaments.

Become a Team Manager:

  1. Register as a Team Manager
  2. Add Team Information
  3. Add Players for the Team
  4. Wait for Approval and once approved
  5. View Upcoming tournaments to apply for them.

Vedor

Registering as a Vendor means that you will only apply for tournaments to sell.

Become a Vendor:

  1. Register as a Vendor
  2. Wait for Approval and once approved
  3. View Upcoming tournaments to apply for them.

All Done!

You are now ready to start using the app.
